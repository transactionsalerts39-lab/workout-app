Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Shrutika Fitness Coaching ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit this website or submit a coaching inquiry.

  • Basic technical usage data like device type and browser data.

How we use your information

  • To respond to inquiries and evaluate coaching fit.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may use trusted third-party tools for form handling, scheduling, analytics, payments, and communication. These providers only receive data needed to perform their function.

Data retention

We retain inquiry and client-related information only as long as needed for business, legal, tax, and operational purposes.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us through the website contact channel.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no online transmission system can be guaranteed as fully secure.

Policy updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. The updated date at the top reflects the latest version.
