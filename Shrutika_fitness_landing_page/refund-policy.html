Refund Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

This Refund Policy outlines how refund requests are handled for Shrutika Fitness Coaching services.

General policy

Because coaching includes reserved time, planning, and digital service delivery, payments are generally non-refundable once services begin.

Before service start

If you cancel before program onboarding starts, you may request a refund minus any processing fees and any non-recoverable administrative charges.

After service start

Once onboarding, strategy delivery, or active coaching has started, refunds are not guaranteed. Any approved exception is at our sole discretion.

How to request a refund review

  • Submit your request in writing through the contact channel.
  • Include your full name, purchase date, and reason for request.
  • Allow up to 7 business days for review and response.

Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback without first contacting us may result in immediate suspension of coaching access while the case is reviewed.

Policy updates

This policy may be updated at any time. The latest version is shown by the date above.
